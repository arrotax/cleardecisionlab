The Three System Failures

Capacity loss isn't random. It follows predictable patterns based on which system is compromised:

Physical System Degradation

Signal: Your nervous system is stuck in emergency mode. HRV is low. Sleep architecture is disrupted. Recovery capacity is offline.

Cost: Decision-making requires more effort. Risk assessment becomes conservative or reckless (no middle ground). Physical symptoms appear: tension, digestive issues, immune suppression.

Cognitive System Degradation

Signal: Attention fragmentation. Task-switching costs accumulate. Working memory is overloaded. You can't hold complex problems in your head anymore.

Cost: Strategic thinking becomes impossible. You default to reactive problem-solving. Planning horizons shrink from quarterly to weekly to daily.

Emotional System Degradation

Signal: Motivation system is offline. Nothing feels meaningful. You're going through motions. Values misalignment creates constant low-grade friction.

Cost: Decision quality drops because you're operating without internal compass. You tolerate situations that violate your values because confronting them requires energy you don't have.
